## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 📦 Supply Chain and Logistics Analytics

A data analytics solution developed to monitor delivery performance, delay risk, regional bottlenecks, and logistics efficiency.

**Key work:**

- Measured on-time and delayed delivery performance
- Analyzed planned versus actual shipping duration
- Identified high-risk shipping modes and regions
- Built operational KPIs and interactive visualizations
- Generated recommendations for reducing delays

**Tools:** Python, Pandas, SQL, Power BI, Streamlit

---

### 🏥 Care Transition Efficiency Analytics

An analytics project studying the movement from custody to care and sponsor placement.

**Key work:**

- Measured transfer and placement efficiency
- Monitored inflow, discharge, and backlog trends
- Identified bottleneck periods
- Developed KPI summaries and interactive dashboards
- Generated recommendations for improving placement outcomes

**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Streamlit, Excel, Data Visualization

---

### 📊 Sales MIS Dashboard

An interactive business dashboard for monitoring sales and operational performance.

**Key work:**

- Created sales, revenue, profit, and regional KPIs
- Designed monthly and category-level reports
- Identified high-performing and low-performing segments
- Automated reporting through structured datasets

**Tools:** Microsoft Excel, Power BI, Power Query

---

### 🏏 IPL Match Winner Prediction

A machine-learning project designed to estimate match outcomes using historical IPL data.

**Key work:**

- Cleaned and prepared historical match data
- Conducted exploratory data analysis
- Engineered important match features
- Trained and evaluated classification models

**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib

---

### 📉 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

A customer analytics project developed to identify churn patterns and retention opportunities.

**Key work:**

- Analyzed customer demographics and service usage
- Identified factors associated with customer churn
- Segmented high-risk customers
- Developed business recommendations for retention

**Tools:** Python, SQL, Excel, Power BI

---

### 💧 Smart Water Tank Monitoring

An IoT-based project for tracking water levels and supporting efficient water management.

**Key work:**

- Monitored water levels through sensors
- Designed alerts for low and high water levels
- Reduced overflow and manual monitoring requirements
- Presented real-time system information

**Tools:** IoT Sensors, Embedded Systems, Dashboarding
